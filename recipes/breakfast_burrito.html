Minute Breakfast Burrito

Description

Prep time: 3 mins

Cook time: 2 mins

Total time: 5 mins

Servings: 1

Yield: 1 burrito

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons salsa
  • 1 slice cheese
  • 1 tortilla

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Crack the eggs into the bowl, add the salsa and stir.
  3. Microwave for 1 min, stir and cook for another minute or until the mixture firms up
  4. Place cheese in the middle of the tortilla and top it with egg mixture.
  5. Wrap it all up like a burrito and it's done!
